Level of cover

This policy entitles you to repair or replacement of your gadget(s). The intention of this policy is to put you back in the same position as immediately prior to the loss or damage. It is not a replacement as new policy.


Term of policy

This is a monthly policy which automatically rolls on for 12 months. You pay your premium in advance monthly and you can stop your policy for a gadget(s) by clicking the stop button next to the gadget description in MyInsuranceChain in your InsurePeople account (for a specific gadget) or by clicking the button next to the monthly total for all gadgets.


Cancellation

You have the right to cancel this policy at any time. You must click, in your MyInsuranceChain in your InsurePeople account, the stop/start button next to the monthly premium amount to cancel the whole policy or the start/stop button against a specific gadget to cancel insurance cover for that gadget only.

You will, for a period of 14 days from the date you first activate the policy on your InsurePeople account, the right to cancel this policy (as a whole) and receive a full refund of premium. To do this, you will need to click the button next to the total monthly premium on your MyInsuranceChain page. Your upfront premium will be fully refunded and your account deactivated.

After the 14 day withdrawal period you pay your premium on a monthly basis and your policy will be cancelled at the next monthly anniversary of the date your policy commenced.

There will be no refund of premium due as the premium paid will have only been in respect of the cover already received.

If a claim has been made by you we will not refund any premium.


In the event of a claim

All claims must be made through your InsurePeople account using the claim button on your MyInsuranceChain, ideally within 48 hours of the discovery of the incident.

For loss and theft we will immediately inform your airtime carrier and your gadget will be disabled. as part of the claim process, you will confirm your airtime details and confirm agreement to locking the gadget.

Reporting your loss (claims procedures)

You must:

  • Notify InsurePeople through the claims button on your account in MyInsuranceChain. Please select the claims button on the same line as the gadget which has been lost, stolen or damaged.
  • Please notify within 48 hours of the discovery of any incident likely to give rise to a claim under this insurance and
  • Report the theft or loss of any gadgets within 24 hours of discovery to your airtime provider and blacklist your handset/tablet.

General

We do not pay any claim:

  • If the serial number of your gadget has been tampered with in any way;
  • For reconnection costs or subscription fees;
  • For uninsured gadgets and phones;
  • If the serial number does not match the serial number of the gadget claimed against.
